Company Overview

Taj Mahal Electrical Contractor Inc. is a trusted electrical contracting firm specializing in comprehensive electrical utility services for residential, commercial and government projects across Pennsylvania. We have a proven track record of successfully delivering high‑quality electrical solutions and pride ourselves on our ability to manage complex projects from start to finish—efficiently, safely and within budget.

We maintain an excellent working relationship with the Department of General Services (DGS) in Pennsylvania and are actively involved in major ongoing DGS projects, including upgrades to the East Wing generator at the Pennsylvania Capitol Building, maintenance garage electrification and the electrical and security system scope at the State Correctional Institute in Muncy County.
